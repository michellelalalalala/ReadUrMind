Speeds up assessment

Instead of having patients lining up for 60 minutes, spending a whole day to examine 6 patients, and waiting for their results for 7 days to reassess, hopitals and doctors can make use of this machine learning model to get an accurate assessment of the patient within seconds.

visibility

Visualization

The output of image processing by the model can be visualized by the patients and doctors directly, with potential problems of lungs highlighted. ChestNut is providing an easier way for patients to get informed of theor health conditions, and help doctors prioritize the service from the doctors, making the maximum impact within units of time.

blur_on

Dense layers

We used 121 layers dense net algorithm with more than 100,000 data to train the model, helping users to get the most accurate results within the least time needed. The technology behind the app help making an impact to the future of cancer diagnosis.

person

Customization

The model integrated personalized patient history as part of the data set, ensuring the quality and accuracy taking the diverse conditions of patients into consideration. We take responsibility of every patient, and generates the most humanized case-by-case results.
